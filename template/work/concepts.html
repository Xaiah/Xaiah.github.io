Work: Game Concept & Illustrations

Honestly speaking I love thinking up concepts during my free time. Whether it be characters, environments, story, or even gameplay, I spend time thinking about at least one of them every day. I carry a sketch book almost everywhere I go to jot down ideas or sketches when I think up anything. Here are some of my concepts at its finest.

Created by Elbin Shin.

K A T A R I N A' S   S K I N   C O N C E P T

I developed this concept skin for the League of Legends champion, Katarina. I thought up this design early this year when the cybernetic skins were being released. The concept was teleportation Tron style. After spending a few days developing it, I had an entire plan set up but didn't have the time to actually do it. Started from a sketch to a color design then school got busy.

N I G H T   E C L I P S E   D R A G O N

I started developing this dragon right after watching How to Train a Dragon. My concept was a dragon that only comes out during the cold temptures of the night. I wanted to create a dragon that was fast and fiere which is why I designed the dragon to have wings as his forearms. I also shaped his head to show ferocity with sharp look. The dragon sleeps during the hot day deep within a mountain caverns, and it emerges during the night to hunt. Again, I did not have the time to sit down and color it because I started working on the environment and never came back to this.

Which leads to my next concept.

C A N Y O N   O F   T H E   E C L I P S E   D R A G O N

After designing the initial dragon, I attempted to create an environment for the dragon. Because it dislikes the heat it needed to be in the mountain ranges where a lot of moisture was present. Which lead me to create this canyon valley. It would be the ideal place for the dragon that i had created. After sketching a few different scenes I finished with this product. Of course, I would like to polish it more than it is now.
