C A R D   G A M E

Client: Kevin Bierre, War Game Seminar Professor

Work: Game Concept & Prototyping

By utilizing a unique set up in map and mechanics, Kristian and I were able to set up this warring card game. The field is set up much like a trading card game's field. Through this and the use of custom made cards I was able to make the most of both worlds. The game is truely unique and puts your strategic abilities to the test.

Created by Elbin Shin and Kristian Abuda.
